# QuizzaBoom - AI-Powered Quiz Platform

## Product Overview
QuizzaBoom is an AI-powered multiplayer quiz platform designed for bars, restaurants, pubs, and entertainment venues. It automates quiz nights from question generation to scoring and results.

## Core Features
- **AI Question Generation**: Generate 30 unique quiz questions in 10 seconds on any theme
- **No App Required**: Players join via QR code scan, works in mobile browser
- **Automatic Scoring**: Real-time scoring and leaderboard updates
- **TV Display Mode**: Professional presentation on venue screens
- **Up to 250 Players**: Support for large venues and events
- **Strategic Jokers**: Players can double points on chosen questions
- **Commercial Breaks**: Customizable intermissions for promotions
- **Email Results**: Automatic results sent to all participants
- **Multi-Site Support**: Run simultaneous quizzes across multiple locations
- **White Label**: Custom branding available on Pro plan

## Target Markets
- **Primary**: UK and Ireland pub quiz market
- **Secondary**: International bars, restaurants, corporate events, educational institutions
- **Venue Types**: Traditional pubs, sports bars, gastropubs, restaurants, hotels, corporate team building

## Pricing (USD)
- **Starter Plan**: $69/month - 5 quizzes/month, up to 250 players, 30-day free trial
- **Pro Plan**: $99/month - Unlimited quizzes, white label, 2 team seats, priority support
- **Free Trial**: 30 days, no credit card required

## Technical Details
- **Platform**: Web-based (React + TypeScript)
- **Deployment**: Vercel

- **AI**: Google Generative AI (Gemini)
- **Languages**: English, French, Spanish, German
- **Browser Support**: All modern browsers, mobile-optimized
